Chapter 1: Digital Well-being

Introduction

This chapter explores the concept of digital well-being and its importance in our increasingly connected world.

Digital Well-being Quiz

1. I often feel anxious when I don't have access to my phone.
2. I spend more time online than I intend to.
3. I feel refreshed and energized after spending time on social media.
4. I often compare myself to others based on what I see online.
5. I make time for activities unrelated to screens every day.

Tips for Digital Well-being

  • Set time limits for social media and other online activities.
  • Take regular breaks from screens.
  • Engage in offline activities that you enjoy.
  • Practice mindfulness and be present in the moment.
  • Connect with others in person.
